Blending Two Images

  1. Start with one image loaded into PhotoWizard.

Load the Second Image

  1. First, select Tools.

  2. Then select Blend Images.

  3. Then, from the Blend Image toolbar, select the second image.

  4. You now have two images to work with. Use touch gestures to resize, rotate, and reposition the foreground image using the image handles.

Adjust Image Settings

  1. Touch the menu item triangle with three dots above it to bring up the four blending options.

Blend Options Description
Reset Discard all changes to the foreground image.
Scale Fit Scale the foreground image to fit the background image.
Swap Swap the foreground and background images.
Aspect Lock Lock the aspect ratio of the foreground image.

  1. Use these blending options to control the behaviour of the foreground image or swap it with the background image.

Choose Blending Mode and Opacity

  1. Now touch the top menu Gear Icon to bring up the blending modes and opacity dialogue.

  2. Experiment with various blending modes and opacities.

  3. When you are happy with your blended image, touch the check mark in the top menu bar to keep your changes.

 

NOTES:

You have creative control over which parts of your images are combined by masking your background image.

 

You have unlimited undo action, so explore different combinations.
