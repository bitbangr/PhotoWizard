Blending Two Images

  1. Start with the background image loaded into PhotoWizard.

Load the Foreground Image

  1. First, select Tools.

  2. Then select Blend Images.

  3. Then, from the Blend Image toolbar, select and load the foreground image.

  4. You now have a foreground and background image to blend.

Resize and Reposition Foreground Image

  1. Touch-drag the box handles to resize and rotate the foreground image.

    Two-finger drag inside the box to reposition the foreground image.

Adjust Foreground Image Settings

  1. Touch the top menu triangle with three dots above it to access the foreground image options.

    Use these to control the foreground image or swap it with the background image.

Blend Options Description
Reset Discard all changes to the foreground image.
Scale Fit Scale the foreground image to fit the background image.
Swap Swap the foreground and background images.
Aspect Lock Lock the aspect ratio of the foreground image.

Select Blending Mode and Opacity

  1. Now touch the top menu Gear icon to bring up the blending modes and opacity dialogue.

    Experiment with these two options until you like your blended image.

Save Your Blended Image

  1. When you're happy with your text edits, touch the check mark in the top menu to keep your results.

Congratulations! You have successfully blended two images!

 

NOTES:

You have creative control over which parts of your images are combined by masking your background image.

 

You have unlimited undo action, so explore different combinations.
