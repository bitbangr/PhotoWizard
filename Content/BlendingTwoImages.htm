Blending Two Images

  1. Start with one image loaded into PhotoWizard.

Load Second Image

 

  1. Then select Tools

  2. Next select Blend Images

  3. Then from Blend Image toolbar select the second image.

  4. You now have two images to work with. Use touch gestures to re-size, rotate, and reposition the foreground image using the image handles

 

Adjust Image Settings

  1. Touch the menu item triangle with three dots above it to bring up the four blending options.

Blend Options Description
Reset Discard all changes to the foreground image.
Scale Fit Scale the foreground image to fit the background image.
Swap Swap the foreground and background images.
Aspect Lock Lock the aspect ratio of the foreground image.

  1. Use these blending options to control the behaviour of the foreground image or swap it with the background image.

 

Chose Blending Mode and Opacity

  1. Now touch the top menu Gear Icon to bring up the blending modes and opacity dialogue.

  2. Experiment with various blending modes and opacity.

  3. When happy with your blended image touch the check mark in the top menu bar to keep your results.

 

NOTES:

The masked portion of your background or starting image will not appear in your blending image.

This gives you creative control over blending portions of your image.
