Adding Text to Images

  1. Start with an image loaded into PhotoWizard.

  1. First select Tools.

  2. Then select Add Text.

  3. Your image is now displayed with default text inside of a box with manipulation handles.

  4. Touch-drag the box handles to re-size and rotate the text. Two-finger drag inside the box to reposition the text.

  5. Select 'T' in the top menu bar or double tap text in box to open text edit window.

    Update your text. Choose left, centre, or right alignment. Select the check mark to keep your changes or the 'x' to discard.

 

Adjust Image Settings

  1. Touch the menu item triangle with three dots above it to bring up the four blending options.

Blend Options Description
Reset Discard all changes to the foreground image.
Scale Fit Scale the foreground image to fit the background image.
Swap Swap the foreground and background images.
Aspect Lock Lock the aspect ratio of the foreground image.

  1. Use these blending options to control the behaviour of the foreground image or swap it with the background image.

 

Chose Blending Mode and Opacity

  1. Now touch the top menu Gear Icon to bring up the blending modes and opacity dialogue.

  2. Experiment with various blending modes and opacity.

  3. When you are happy with your blended image touch the check mark in the top menu bar to keep your results.

 

NOTES:

You have creative control over which parts of your images are combined by masking your background image.

 

You have an unlimited undo action so feel free to explore different combinations
